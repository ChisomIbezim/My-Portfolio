Act report¶

This project analyzes the WeRateDogs dataset, a page from tweeter. WeRateDogs is a Twitter account that rates peoples dogs with a humorous comment about the dog. The account was started in 2015 by a college student Matt Nelson. This account has attracted 8.8 million+ followers ever since.

Three datasets were used for this data analysis project (tweeter-archive-enhanced.csv, image-predictions.tsv and tweet-json.txt), and each were gathered in different ways given their different sources. Here, I will be sharing my insights using data visualizations. For this project, I answered 3 questions about the datasets used for this project. These questions are:

  1. Q1: On average, which dog stage is most liked?
  2. Q2: Which dog is most liked?
  3. Q3: What is the relationship between retweet_count and favorite_count? These questions may not be very deep, but they reveal some things about the dataset.
